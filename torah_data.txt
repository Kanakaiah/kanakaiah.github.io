# 📖 BOOK 1: GENESIS (50 Chapters)

## ✦ Image Word: GARDEN | Key Word: BEGINNING | Subtitle: *Where Everything Began and Everything Broke*

## ✦ Big Structure
```
Chapters  1–11   =  PRIMEVAL     (Creation, Fall, Flood, Babel)
Chapters  12–25  =  ABRAHAM      (Call, covenant, sacrifice)
Chapters  26–36  =  JACOB        (Deception, wrestling, reconciliation)
Chapters  37–50  =  JOSEPH       (Pit to palace — family restored)
```

Think: **11 → 14 → 11 → 14**

promised in 3:15 moving through every generation toward the cross.*

## ✦ Image Word: BURNING BUSH | Key Word: REDEMPTION | Subtitle: *The God Who Descends to Set His People Free*

## ✦ Big Structure
```
Chapters  1–12   =  DELIVERANCE   (Slavery, plagues, Passover)
Chapters  13–18  =  WILDERNESS    (Sea crossing, manna, water)
Chapters  19–24  =  COVENANT      (Sinai, Ten Commandments)
Chapters  25–40  =  TABERNACLE    (Instructions and construction)
```
Think: **12 → 6 → 6 → 16**
*The book moves from slavery to sanctuary — from Pharaoh's
house to God's house. The second half is all tabernacle
because God's goal was never just to get them out of Egypt —
it was to dwell among them.*

# 📖 BOOK 3: LEVITICUS (27 Chapters)

## ✦ Image Word: ALTAR | Key Word: HOLY | Subtitle: *Come Near — But Only Through Blood and Fire*

## ✦ Big Structure
```
Chapters  1–7    =  OFFERINGS     (Five types of sacrifice)
Chapters  8–10   =  PRIESTHOOD    (Aaron consecrated — Nadab and Abihu)
Chapters  11–16  =  PURITY        (Clean and unclean — Day of Atonement)
Chapters  17–27  =  HOLINESS CODE ("Be holy for I am holy")
```
Think: **7 → 3 → 6 → 11**
*Leviticus answers one question: How does a sinful people
approach a holy God? The entire book is God's answer —
through sacrifice, through priesthood, through atonement,
through holiness. Every chapter points to Christ.*

## ✦ The Torah as One Unbroken Story

```
Genesis      →   The world is MADE and BROKEN
Exodus       →   God DESCENDS to REDEEM His people
Leviticus    →   God TEACHES them HOW to APPROACH Him
Numbers      →   They FAIL the approach — forty years
Deuteronomy  →   Moses PREPARES the next generation to TRY AGAIN
```

The Torah ends with Israel on the edge of the Promised Land and Moses dead on a mountain — still looking forward. It is deliberately **unfinished** — because the story of redemption will take another Testament to complete.
